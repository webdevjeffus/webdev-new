About this app

This app shows the local time and weather conditions. It updates the time every minute, and the weather every 10 minutes. Weather information comes from the OpenWeatherMap.org API.

This is a work in progress. It is currently retrieving and displaying all necessary data, but it has not yet been styled for looks. Check back for an updated version soon.
